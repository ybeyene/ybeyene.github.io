About Me

Hi! I’m Yared — a UX Researcher & Designer with a Master’s in Information from the University of Michigan, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I focus on understanding human behavior to design intuitive digital experiences.

🎯 My Design Philosophy

Design begins with empathy. I believe every interaction should reflect understanding, inclusivity, and simplicity — transforming complexity into clarity.

💡 My Skills

User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability Testing, Information Architecture, SQL Analytics, and UX Writing.

🤝 Collaboration

I love working with cross-functional teams and mentoring others in design thinking and problem-solving methodologies.
