About Me

Hello! I’m Yared Beyene — a passionate UX Researcher and HCI professional with a Master’s in Information from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I believe in designing human-centered experiences that connect people, technology, and purpose.


My background blends Human-Computer Interaction, Information Analytics, and UX Research. I focus on transforming insights into impactful design decisions that enhance accessibility, usability, and emotional connection in digital products.


Outside of design, I enjoy reading about emerging technologies, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and mentoring others interested in UX. My mission is to design systems that make people’s lives easier and more meaningful.
