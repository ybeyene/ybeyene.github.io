Information Dashboard

Transforming data into insights through visual design and UX clarity.

Overview

The goal of this project was to design an intuitive dashboard for organizational analytics, allowing decision-makers to easily understand performance metrics and trends. My role included user research, wireframing, interface design, and usability testing.

Tools & Methods

  • Figma
  • Adobe XD
  • User Interviews
  • Affinity Mapping
  • Usability Testing

Design Process

1. Research

Conducted interviews with analysts and managers to understand their workflow and challenges when accessing business data.

2. Design

Created low-fidelity wireframes to establish hierarchy, followed by high-fidelity prototypes using a modular layout for scalability and clarity.

3. Testing

Conducted usability sessions where participants interacted with prototype dashboards. Improved clarity of chart labels and color accessibility.

Outcome

The final design reduced data retrieval time by 40% and increased user satisfaction. It was later adopted as a standard template for internal reporting.
